Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Is ordering online secure?

A. Yes. We take the utmost care with the information that you provide us when placing an order on our website (or through any other means). All of the information you provide during the ordering process is restricted to our staff, and we make sure that all of our employees are up-to-date on our security and privacy policies. If you have further questions about the security of ordering online from the Trick Storee, please feel free to e-mail us at support@trickstoree.com.

Q. How do I place my order?

A.

  • Click on the product you want to buy.
  • Click on “Add To Cart” option.
  • You can add multiple products to the cart by opening different products and adding them to the cart.
  • Click “Cart” on the menu bar.
  • Scroll down and click “Proceed to checkout”.
  • Fill in all billing details and check Order detail (including total cost).
  • Click on the “Place Order” button below the form.
  • Your order is placed, and you will receive a confirmation email with your order details.
  • We offer Cash On Delivery (COD) or online payment via third-party payment gateway.

Q. How do I cancel an order on Trick Storee?

A. To cancel your order, you can call our helpline at +92-335-6333396 or +92-302-1268381 during office hours. You can also cancel the order during the final confirmation call.

Q. What is your returns policy?

A. For return policy, please visit the Return Policy page.

Q. What is your refund policy?

A. For refund policy, please visit the Refund Policy page.

Q. What is your shipping cost?

A. Our shipping cost is Rs 250/-

Q. Which service do you use for shipping?

A. We use Post Ex Courier Services for shipping.

Q. What is your time to deliver?

A. We deliver within 2-3 working days after the confirmation call.

Q. How do I order an out-of-stock product?

A. You can pre-order any product that is out of stock by contacting us via call or email at support@trickstoree.com.

Q. How can I get free shipping on my order?

A. On purchases over Rs 4999, you can select the "FREE SHIPPING" option (except on Flash Sale products).
