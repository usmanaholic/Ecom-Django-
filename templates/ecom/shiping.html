Shipping Policy

Shipping Policy for Physical Products

Order Processing Time

Once your order is confirmed, we aim to process it within 24 hours and arrange pickup with our third-party courier service. Please note that delivery times may vary based on the destination. Typical delivery estimates are as follows:

  • Same City (Multan): Expected delivery within 24-48 business hours
  • Major Cities in Pakistan: Expected delivery within 48-72 business hours
  • Remote Areas (e.g., Gilgit, Swat): Expected delivery within 5-6 business days

Shipping Destinations

We ship exclusively within Pakistan, covering over 750 destinations nationwide.

Shipping Method & Fees

We offer standard delivery across Pakistan, with a flat delivery fee of 250 PKR per order. Estimated delivery times are as outlined above, depending on your location.

Free Shipping Offers

We occasionally offer free shipping promotions, such as on orders of three or more items. These offers are available for limited times only and may vary.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns and exchanges on physical products within 5 days of delivery. For more details, please review our complete Return, Refund, and Exchange Policy.

Shipping Policy for Digital Products

Delivery Method

All digital products are delivered via email. To ensure seamless delivery, please provide a correct email address at the time of purchase. If an incorrect email address is provided, we will reach out via phone to confirm the correct details.

Digital Product Availability

Some digital products are available for download, while others are provided directly via email without a download link.

Returns & Refunds for Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital products, we do not accept returns or provide refunds. For more information, please review our Return, Refund, and Exchange Policy.
